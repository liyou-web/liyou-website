👤 ABOUT ME

I am a Senior Researcher at the Asia-Japan Research Institute, Ritsumeikan University, Japan. My research primarily focuses on solar energy and solar radiation, peer-to-peer energy sharing, virtual power plants, and energy transition analysis. I also have a keen interest in building energy systems, exploring ways to optimize energy consumption and enhance sustainability.

My recent projects include developing knowledge-based solar radiation prediction models, integrating hybrid deep learning frameworks for solar radiation forecasting, and conducting multi-scale carbon footprint analyses for zero-carbon communities.

For more information or to discuss potential collaborations, please feel free to contact me at liyou@fc.ritsumei.ac.jp or liyou.rs@outlook.com (Permanent Address).
